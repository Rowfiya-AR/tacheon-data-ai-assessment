   # Product Brief: Daily Performance Summary

   ## Problem
   Marketing analysts spend 2+ hours daily pulling data from 5+ platforms to report performance.

   ## Users
   Marketing analysts and managers at mid-size e-commerce brands.

   ## Solution
   Automated daily summary with key metrics, anomalies, and insights delivered via Slack/email.

   ## Success Metrics
   1. Time saved per analyst per day
   2. % of teams using it daily after 2 weeks
   3. Reduction in manual reporting errors
